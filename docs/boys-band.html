About

We're wild and crazy

Welcome to the official website of the Wix Boys, a young boys band from Alabama, virtuoso guitar musicians with vocal harmony and beat boxing. We began in 2013 and have already tried ourselves in numerous festivals and acoustic evenings. The Wix Boys were handed the award of “Young artist of the year” at 2014 Alabama Blues Awards ceremony and are ready to perform all around the country, in big cities and small towns and surrounding areas.

We are crazy about music and make it with all our love and passion. We play an unusual high energy mix of blues, funk, jazz, soul, old-school rock, swing, 90s/80s/70s hits and more – with our virtuoso guitars. We're excited and full of energy, ready to make a big name for ourselves. We transfer our feelings to guitar playing to explore new life in music. Our singles create a shimmering world of deep sound. Listen and enjoy!
